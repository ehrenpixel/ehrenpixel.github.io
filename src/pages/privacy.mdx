## 2. Data Collection and Purposes
* **Anonymous Use:** The game Tiltsprint can be played without registration. In this case, no personal data is processed to identify the user.
* **Registration:** If you choose to create a user account, we collect your **username** and **email address**. This data is used solely for account management and cross-platform synchronization of game progress.
* **Technical Data:** When accessing our backend, your **IP address** is recorded in server logs for security and operational purposes. These logs are not evaluated for marketing or analytical tracking.
* **Device Information:** We collect hardware information (device model, operating system version) to optimize Tiltsprint's performance and compatibility for your specific platform.
* **Local Storage:** Game saves are stored locally on your device unless synchronized via an account.

## 3. Third-Party Services and Platform Integration
Tiltsprint offers optional integration with the following services to link accounts and sync achievements:
* **Google Play Games Services** (Google Ireland Limited, Ireland)
* **Apple Game Center** (Apple Inc., USA)
* **Steam** (Valve Corporation, USA)

When using these services, platform-specific IDs are processed to track achievements and verify your identity. Please refer to the privacy policies of the respective providers.

## 4. Hosting and Data Location
All data collected through registration and synchronized game saves are stored on self-operated servers located in **Germany**. Your data is not sold or shared with third parties, except for the platform integrations mentioned in Section 3.

## 5. Account Deletion and Data Retention
### How to Request Deletion
Users can request the deletion of their account and all associated data at any time through the following methods:
1. **Email:** Send a request from your registered email address to **support@ehrenpixel.com**.

### Types of Data Deleted
Upon a deletion request, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:
* Your email address and username.
* All synchronized game saves and progress data.
* Platform linking identifiers.

### Data Retention
* **Immediate Deletion:** Most data is deleted immediately upon request.
* **Log Data:** IP addresses stored in technical logs are automatically purged after 30 days.
* **Legal Requirements:** We only retain data beyond the deletion request if required by law (e.g., for tax or accounting purposes) or to resolve ongoing disputes.

## 6. User Rights
You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact the developer at the email address provided above.
