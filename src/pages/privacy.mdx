## 2. Data Collection and Purposes
* **Anonymous Use:** The game can be played without registration. In this case, no personal data is processed to identify the user.
* **Registration:** If you choose to create a user account, we collect your **username** and **email address**. This data is used solely for account management and cross-platform synchronization of game progress.
* **Technical Data:** When accessing our backend, your **IP address** is recorded in server logs for security and operational purposes. These logs are not evaluated for marketing or analytical tracking.
* **Device Information:** We collect hardware information (device model, operating system version) to optimize game performance and compatibility for your specific platform.
* **Local Storage:** Game saves are stored locally on your device unless synchronized via an account.

## 3. Third-Party Services and Platform Integration
The game offers optional integration with the following services to link accounts and sync achievements:
* **Google Play Games Services** (Google Ireland Limited, Ireland)
* **Apple Game Center** (Apple Inc., USA)
* **Steam** (Valve Corporation, USA)

When using these services, platform-specific IDs are processed to track achievements and verify your identity. Please refer to the privacy policies of the respective providers.

## 4. Hosting and Data Location
All data collected through registration and synchronized game saves are stored on self-operated servers located in **Germany**. Your data is not sold or shared with third parties, except for the platform integrations mentioned in Section 3.

## 5. User Rights
You have the right to:
* Request access to your stored personal data.
* Request the correction of inaccurate data.
* Request the deletion of your account and associated data.
* Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address provided above.

## 6. Data Retention
Data is stored as long as the account remains active. If you request account deletion, all associated data (email, username, synchronized saves) will be removed from our servers immediately, unless legal retention periods apply.
