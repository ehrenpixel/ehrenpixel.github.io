# Software that earns its name.

EHRENPiXEL is an independent software studio built on a simple belief: **software should honor the people who use it.**

We don’t sell your data, obscure our pricing, or ship things we wouldn’t use ourselves. No tricks. No dark patterns. No nonsense. Just honest, handcrafted software — games and tools — driven by craft, not growth hacks.

Whether it’s a game or a SaaS product, we hold everything to the same standard: respect your time, protect your privacy, do the job well.

Every pixel we place, every line of code we write, is guided by one principle:

**Honor the user. Earn the trust.**

_Independent. Crafted in Germany. That’s EHRENPiXEL._
